LinkedIn Usage Guidelines

  • Company Profile Logo: Use 300×300px square version
  • Cover Photo: Use 1128×191px horizontal banner with logo + text
  • Post Images: Use 1200×628px for shared content
  • Minimum Size: Logo should never be smaller than 24px in height
  • Background: Logo works best on white or very light backgrounds
  • Clear Space: Always maintain space around logo equal to the logo's height
